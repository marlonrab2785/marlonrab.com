About me

Turning complexity
into clarity.

I'm a Product Designer and UI/UX Design Lead who specializes in making dense business logic, edge cases, and enterprise workflows feel simple—so users can just get things done.

With 14+ years across the design stack, I've found my edge in enterprise SaaS—where product, engineering, and business collide, and clarity is not optional.

At LoanPro, I built a design system from the ground up, led a rebrand across core product areas, and played a leading role in key strategic partnerships. I leverage AI-assisted design workflows to move quickly without cutting corners.

I'm not a developer, but I speak engineering fluently—bridging the gap between design and code.
